### Pasta & pumpkin sauce


- 1-2 TBsp olive oil
- garlic, peeled and chopped
- 500g pumpkin, chopped small
- 200ml single cream
- 1 tsp parmesan
- basil
- salt & pepper


fry garlic in olive oil until soft.   
Add pumpkin, cover with water, and simmer until soft.  
blend with salt & pepper.  
stir in cream.  
pour over pasta with fresh basil.
